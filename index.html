Madelyne Oliver

"Madelyne has a BA in Art History and Anthropology from the University of California, Irvine and an MBA from San Francisco State University. She has over five years of experience in email marketing, marketing automation, content marketing, and lead acquisition working for large B2B and B2C companies like Monster Job Worldwide, Gartner, Skyventure, Inc., and Yesler, Inc. She has also built up marketing departments for small startups in the Ecommerce space. Over the past few years, she has also worked in the entrepreneurial world, providing marketing consulting for small companies, helping them build recurring revenue through email, automation, and strategy. When not wearing her marketing hat, Madelyne wears her art therapy hat developing courses and organizing workshops designed to help working professionals overcome anxiety, depression, and PTSD outside of their jobs. As an art therapy coach, she is trained to guide people from these dark head spaces into a realm of happiness and fulfillment. She believes in holistic healthy daily practices that are complementary to western medicine, in order to manage mental health. She is most passionate about bringing accessible health and care for the human mind. "
